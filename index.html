Social Issues Mentality


What is it?


The collection of mental frameworks and attitudes that shape how individuals and groups perceive, analyze, and respond to social problems.

How does it Impact people


Social Issues can effect people through both external impacts, like stress and discrimination, and internal influences, such as conformity and changes in self-perception.

How to solve Social Issues


Focus on individual coping mechanisms like developing coping skills, practicing mindfulness and gratitude, and engaging in activities that provide a sense of meaning and purpose.
